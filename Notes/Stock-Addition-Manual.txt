Process for IPO stock
=====================
I) On the day of IPO
1) Insert stock name to AAB/data/name-ttl.csv
	--So evening process can get the price to AAB\data\price.csv
2) Call AAB/database/Stock-Addition.ipyrnb
	-- To create records for tables nameall & stockname in MySql stock.db

II) On the day after IPO
1) Call AAB/ruby/crt-tickers-fm-fs.rb -o 
2) Run following process for 3 databases to insert new ticker record:
	3.1) Change dir to PortLt & rails runner db/crt_tickers.rb
	3.2) Change dir to PortMy & rails runner db/crt_tickers.rb
	3.3) Change dir to PortPg & rails runner db/crt_tickers.rb
3) Call every morning process C:/AAB/Morn-Proc.bat

Process for non-IPO stock
=====================
1) Insert stock name to AAB/data/name-ttl.csv
	--So evening process can get the price to AAB\data\price.csv

2) Call AAB/database/Stock-Addition.ipyrnb
	-- To create records for tables nameall & stockname in MySql stock.db

3) Call AAB/ruby/crt-tickers-fm-fs.rb -o
 
4) Call Morn-Proc.bat
 
5) Run following process for 3 databases to insert new ticker record to 
   stock and consensus tables
	3.1) Change dir to PortLt & rails runner db/crt_all.rb
	3.2) Change dir to PortMy & rails runner db/crt_all.rb
	3.3) Change dir to PortPg & rails runner db/crt_all.rb
